Thank you for reviewing Alma – Meditation & Wellness.

APP OVERVIEW
Alma is a mental wellness app offering guided meditations, breathing exercises, daily reflection articles, mood tracking and an AI-powered wellness companion. The app is primarily in Portuguese (pt-BR) with English also supported.

ACCOUNT / LOGIN
The app supports both email/password login and Google Sign-In via Firebase Authentication.

Demo account credentials are provided in the App Store Connect App Review Information fields (Sign-In Information section). The account has a complete profile with onboarding finished.

SANDBOX TESTER — IN-APP PURCHASES (Guideline 2.1)
A dedicated Apple Sandbox Tester account is configured for IAP testing. Credentials and step-by-step instructions for testing the subscription purchase flow and account deletion are provided in the App Store Connect Notes field.

Subscription Product IDs:
- Monthly: com.almaapp.app.premium_monthly (7-day free trial)
- Annual:  com.almaapp.app.premium_annual  (7-day free trial)

IN-APP PURCHASES
The app offers a monthly and annual subscription (Premium plan) via Apple StoreKit. Subscriptions unlock the full content library and AI companion. The subscription paywall appears after onboarding. You can dismiss it and continue with the free tier.

PERMISSIONS
- Notifications: requested optionally for daily wellness reminders. Not required to use the app.
- No camera, microphone, location or contacts access is required.

CONTENT RATING
The app is appropriate for ages 12+. All content is focused on mental wellness, self-care and positive habit formation. There is no violent, sexual or harmful content.
