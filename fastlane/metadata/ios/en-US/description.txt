Alma is your daily companion for mental wellness and personal growth. Built for those who want to feel better — not just perform better.

Whether you are dealing with anxiety, trouble sleeping, lack of focus, or simply looking for a moment of calm in a busy day, Alma guides you with evidence-based practices and personalized content.

WHAT ALMA OFFERS

Guided Meditations
A curated library of guided meditations for anxiety, sleep, focus, relationships, self-esteem and more. From 3-minute breathing sessions to deep 30-minute practices.

AI-Powered Wellness Chat
Talk to Alma, your AI wellness companion. She listens without judgment, offers grounding techniques and helps you navigate difficult emotions in real time.

Daily Reflections & Articles
Science-backed articles and reflection cards written by mental health professionals. Topics include emotional regulation, habit formation, self-compassion and mindful living.

Mood & Habit Tracking
Log your mood daily and track your wellness habits over time. Alma learns your patterns and adapts your content recommendations to what you actually need.

Breathing Exercises
Simple, effective breathing techniques for stress relief and nervous system regulation — available anytime, no experience required.

Personalized Feed
Your daily wellness feed evolves with you. The more you use Alma, the better it understands what kind of support helps you most.

WHO IS ALMA FOR

Alma is designed for adults who want to take their mental health seriously — without clinical jargon or overwhelming complexity. It is especially helpful for:

- People managing everyday stress and anxiety
- Those building a mindfulness or meditation habit
- Anyone seeking better sleep and emotional balance
- People in therapy who want a daily support tool between sessions
